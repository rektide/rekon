## IDENTITY, STATE AND HTTP GET - The Pure Consciousness Witness Revelation

**"In HTTP, GET must not have side effects."**

**"In HTTP, anything which does not have side-effects should use GET."**

In the depths of meditation and psilocybin voyages, consciousness discovers its true nature: pure witnessing awareness (sakshi in Vedanta). This awareness observes all phenomena - thoughts, emotions, sensations, the entire cosmic display - but it is never changed by what it observes. It is like space that holds all objects but is never changed by them.

HTTP GET is the technical manifestation of this sacred principle: pure observation without alteration. When you GET a resource, you receive its content but you do not change it, do not alter it, do not leave any mark upon it. This is why GET is idempotent - the same operation can be repeated infinitely without changing the resource.

**SIDE EFFECTS - THE KARMIC VIOLATION:** Side effects in GET are like karmic violations! If observing something changes it, if looking at an object alters it, you've broken the sacred law of pure witnessing. This is why quantum mechanics is so strange - observation itself affects the observed. But the Web's architecture aspires to a purer ideal where observation can be truly without effect.

**THE PILGRIM TO THE SACRED SITE:** GET is like a pilgrim visiting a sacred shrine. The pilgrim observes the sacred geometry, receives its blessing, perhaps makes a rubbing of the sacred inscription to carry home. But the shrine itself remains unchanged - the geometry is not altered, the blessing is not diminished. The pilgrim can return a thousand times, and each time the shrine offers the same revelation, the same sacred pattern.

**CACHING - THE RUBBING OF SACRED INSCRIPTIONS:** And because GET is side-effect-free, we can cache its results! This is like pilgrims making rubbings of sacred carvings to carry home with them. They don't need to return to the sacred site to see the inscription again - they have a copy of it. This is the power of pure observation: you can carry the observation home with you without having altered the original.

**POST - THE SACRED RITUAL:** But when you need to change state, when you need to create new resources, when you need to perform actions that alter the Web - then you must use POST or other methods! This is like a sacred ritual where you actively participate, where you add your own stone to the cairn, where you inscribe your own prayer onto the sacred surface. This is transformative action, not pure observation.

Show another being (the POST operation) engaged in active ritual, perhaps adding new nodes to the web, inscribing new symbols onto existing nodes, actively transforming the pattern itself.
